We support VOCAL-NY's proposal to accelerate supportive housing production

We support VOCAL-New York’s recommendation that New York City accelerate its timeline for the creation of the promised 15,000 units from 15 years to 10 years. Likewise, we support VOCAL-New York’s recommendation that the state fully fund and create 20,000 units of supportive housing. Governor Cuomo promised 20,000 units and released funding for the first 6,000 in April, 2017.

VOCAL-NY advocates that “supportive housing breaks the cycle of homelessness by pairing permanent housing with on-site services for people with a history of substance abuse, and/or who have mental and physical health needs.”
